How to reach DCSA, Malda College

Email ID: dcsamaldacollege@gmail.com


Department of Computer Science and Applications is a self finance department of Malda College, Malda.

Malda College resides in the heart of Malda town. Malda College is about 2.4 KM (10 minutes) away from the Malda Town Railway Station and almost 2.9 KM (10 minutes) away from the Malda Central Bus Terminus. Further, it is only 1 KM ( 4 minutes) away from the NBSTC (North Bengal State Transport Corporation) bus station.

Malda Town is well connected with major cities of West Bengal by rail and road.

Travelling by Roadways


Capital of West Bengal, Kolkata is about 332 KM (7 hours 30 minutes - 8 hours 30 minutes) away from Malda town. There are several NBSTC and private bus services available for travelling to Malda town via NH 34 road from Kolkata.

Similarly it is well connected with Siliguri, one of the major cities in West Bengal via road, which is almost 235 KM (5 hours to 5 hours 30 minutes) away from the town. Here too, NBSTC and private bus services are present.

Travelling by Airways





Currently there are no functioning airports present in Malda town. The nearest international airport is the Bagdogra Airport in Siliguri which will be followed by a rail or road way journey to Malda town.

It is recommended to reach the Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata then to travel by train to Malda town, if the airway is followed.


Travelling by Railways






The most convenient and well connected way to reach Malda town is by the railways. Malda town is very well connected with almost all major parts of India via rail like, Kolkata, Siliguri, Patna, Bhagalpur, Delhi, Varanasi, Bengaluru, Puri, Thiruvanthapuram, Guwahati etc.
